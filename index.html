About Us

SRN Metha School offers comprehensive education from kindergarten to high school, including PU college. The campus features state-of-the-art sports facilities, ensuring a holistic development environment for students. We strive to provide affordable, qualitative, and morally sound education for every child.

Read more

Our Mission

To foster a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to develop academically, socially, and personally. We aim to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that prepares students to be lifelong learners, responsible citizens, and leaders in their communities. We envision a future where every student has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Core Values

Integrity, Respect, Excellence, Community, and Innovation. These values guide our actions and decisions, ensuring we provide a balanced and inclusive education to all our students.

Our Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library provides a quiet space for students to study and a diverse collection of books and resources to support their learning and encourage a love for reading.

Laboratories

We have state-of-the-art science and computer labs that allow students to gain hands-on experience and practical skills, which are crucial for understanding theoretical concepts.

Sports Complex

Our sports complex includes facilities for various sports, promoting physical fitness and teamwork. Students can participate in sports like football, basketball, cricket, and athletics.

Art and Music Rooms

Creative expression is encouraged through our dedicated art and music rooms, where students can explore their talents and develop their artistic and musical skills.

Auditorium

Our auditorium is a hub for various school events, including assemblies, performances, and guest lectures. It is equipped with modern audio-visual technology.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers nutritious and balanced meals, ensuring that students have access to healthy food options during their school day.

Curriculum

Kindergarten

Our kindergarten program focuses on the holistic development of children through play-based learning, ensuring a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

Primary School

The primary school curriculum emphasizes core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and English, while also incorporating arts, physical education, and social studies to provide a well-rounded education.

High School

Our high school program prepares students for higher education and future careers through a rigorous academic curriculum, including advanced courses in STEM, humanities, and languages.

PU College

The PU college offers pre-university courses in various streams, including science, commerce, and arts, designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for university education.

Extracurricular Activities

We provide a wide range of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports, and cultural programs, to help students develop their interests and talents outside the classroom.

Special Programs

Our special programs include student exchange initiatives, internships, and community service projects that offer students unique learning experiences and opportunities for personal growth.
